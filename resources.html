Educational Resources
From Our YouTube Channel

BIOME is dedicated to creating an open space for anyone interested in getting involved in bioengineering, and we want to give every student the tools necessary to pursue biology-inspired projects. Our YouTube channel has educational resources to help you gain skills and knowledge you need. You can learn a variety of wet and dry lab techniques, and hear innovative talks from experts across the field.

Check out our content and subscribe to our YouTube channel so you don't miss any of our videos! If there is a video you want to see us make, or a topic you want us to cover, let us know about it in the Google Form linked below! You can also email the exec team if you want to be involved with creating videos - we are open to help from anyone.
